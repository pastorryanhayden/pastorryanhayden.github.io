This week, I’m going to be posting every day about sins the Bible talk about, but that you rarely hear any Independent Baptists mention. You can find all kinds of Independent Baptist sermons on immodesty, on alcohol, on failure to go soul winning, on worldliness, on abortion and homosexuality (and I’ve preached on all of those things) but if your listening for sermons against:

  • Glorying in men
  • Strife
  • Envy
  • Respecting Persons
  • Judgmentalism


Crickets.

I’ll look at one each day.
