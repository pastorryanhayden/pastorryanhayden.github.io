In the video above, I show preachers my method for creating sermon manuscripts and turning them into very useable kindle ebooks using free tools.  Notes and links on the software I use are listed below:

About Markdown:


Markdown is a plain text syntax for writers that easily converts to HTML.  It takes minutes to learn and is extremely useful.  To learn more about markdown, visit http://daringfireball.net/projects/markdown/basics.

Software Notes:


iA Writer: The Writer's plain text editor I use.  (Mac Only)

http://www.iawriter.com/mac/

Calibre: Free Ebook management and conversion software. (Mac, Windows, and Linux)

http://calibre-ebook.com/

Send to Kindle: Simple utility to send documents and ebooks to the kindle or kindle app of your choice.

http://www.amazon.com/gp/sendtokindle
