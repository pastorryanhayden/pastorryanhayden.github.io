I’m going to do something a little unorthodox here and post this on my blog.
I’m looking for someone to come and help us with our youth ministry. Specifically, I’m looking for someone who:

  • Has a clear testimony of salvation.
  • Is an independent Baptist.
  • Has been called to the ministry.
  • Has graduated from Bible college.
  • Is either married or engaged.
  • Is willing to start from scratch.
  • Is willing to work with younger children.
  • Is willing to be bi-vocational.
  • Is willing to start on a probational basis.


 

I cannot promise much, but I can promise that our church is a great place to minister.

If you are interested, please send your resume to pastorryanhayden@gmail.com. If you know someone you think would be a good fit, please encourage them to send me their resume.
