Don't try to kick them out of office

Don't call them names or rant on them

Don't blame them for all your problems

Don't treat them like the enemy

Treat them like people

People Christ died for

People being defeated by the same enemy that you are

You don't win people with politics

You don't win people with Facebook memes

You don't win people by calling them names

You don't win people with hate

You win people with love

So love them

Love them by walking across the room

Love them by sharing a meal

Love them by listening

Love them by sharing Christ with them

Love them still when they reject Him

They are not the enemy

They are deceived by the enemy

Love will help them see that

Stop trying to make casualties

Start trying to make converts

Everything is downstream of hearts and minds

So win hearts and minds
