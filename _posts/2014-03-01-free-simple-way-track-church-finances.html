Basically, you:

  • Download the free general ledger spreadsheet from freechurchacounting.com
  • Open the spreadsheet in google drive. (and save it as a drive file.)
  • Share the spreadsheet with whoever acts as your financial secretary.
  • Enjoy the ability to have an up to date overview of your church finances anywhere you have an internet connection.
